Message from the Secretary General

Dear Citizens of the world,

On behalf of the secretariat, organizing committee and staff, it gives me great pleasure in welcoming you to Ravenshaw University Model United Nations 2016, which will be held from the 8th -10th of July, 2016 at Ravenshaw University, Cuttack.

 The conference has been structured with four committees and the International Press, and our belief is that each committee offers something new and unprecedented in the Indian MUN Circuit. Our aim is to encourage the participants to think even after the three days of debate, so that ideas and thoughts do not remain in the four walls of the councils. We believe that we will be truly successful, when each and every participant at the MUN goes back, still thinking, with a different view on a global issue, and a desire to make a difference.

Ravenshaw University MUN lends its voice to the various ideas of the Gen Y and prepares them for their future role of sculpting a superior society. Our conference is a platform for deliberation and debate which helps young adults to develop their debating prowess, logical reasoning, diplomacy and social skills. It delves deep into several issues which plague humanity, and determinedly strives to come up with innovative solutions. In this edition we present to you 4 exciting committees- UN Charter Review Conference, World Humanitarian Summit UN Women and All India Political Parties Meet.

With a steadfast Organising Committee working ceaselessly to bring out the best at RUMUN, a highly accomplished and decorated Executive Board providing their valuable counsel for your holistic progress and an environment so stirring that it inspires you to make possible the impossible, Ravenshaw University MUN 2016 is bound to be an exalting event that will leave you with a lot of experience in the area of International Affairs.

 I look forward to seeing you all in July.

Let’s change the world, one MUN at a time.
