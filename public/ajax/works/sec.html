Message from the Secretary General

Hello,
As we get ready to usher in the New Year with great fanfare and celebration, it is natural that we look forward to 2017 being the harbinger of a new phase of success, prosperity and well-being in our lives. In the same vein, a group of young and vibrant CETians takes great honour to be a part of an endeavour that you are sure to cherish- CET MUN 2017. Being one of the oldest MUNs in Odisha whose first edition was organised way back in 2013, CET MUN pioneered the healthy culture of MUNs in this circuit. Now, its back in full vigour and in a brand new Avatar. Spread over three days of stimulating discussions and dialogue, the three committees being simulated in this edition are sure to define constructive diplomacy. It is our abiding commitment to ensure quality of debate and provide a grand platform to over 250 delegates to articulate themselves and prove their skills. Our Secretariat constantly strives to offer a memorable experience- be it within the council or outside it. The council proceedings apart, the Delegates should look forward to multiple social nights with the star night sure to take everybody's breath away! Too much revelation for now! As the conference approaches, we would be giving you a thousand reasons to honour us and enrich yourselves with your presence. Once again, a Hearty Welcome to CET MUN 2017. We just can't wait to have you here soon!
